About Us

We are dedicated to provide the last touch in interior design and construction. We pride ourselves on understanding materials and delivering high-quality finishes that are aesthetically pleasing, durable, and up to industry standards.

Our Services

At Vertex, we specialize in providing premium finishing solutions for commercial, residential, and medical spaces. From hygienic hospital finishes to custom designs and landscaping, our services combine durability, functionality, and aesthetic excellence to bring your vision to life.

Hospital & Medical Finishes

We understand the importance of hygiene in medical environments. Our finishes are designed for easy cleaning and meet the highest sanitary standards, without compromising on aesthetics.

Landscaping

We offer comprehensive landscaping services that enhance the aesthetic and functional value of outdoor spaces, ensuring harmony between the interior and exterior environments.

Consultation and Material Expertise

Our deep understanding of materials allows us to advise clients on the best options for their projects and drive the needed material from their raw state to a flawless finish.

Custom Finishes for Commercial & Residential Spaces

Vertex delivers solutions that reflect the unique character and vision of every project, combining beauty with lasting durability.
